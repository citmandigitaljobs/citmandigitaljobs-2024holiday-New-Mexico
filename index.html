New Mexico

Amtrak from El Paso to Tucson

Through Dona Ana, Luna, Grant and Hidalgo Counties

The Texas Eagle took us from El Paso to Tucson, via New Mexico. We travelled past a small canal of the Rio Grande, then a long and imposing stretch of US-Mexico border wall, followed by flat plains and then the Santa Rita and Santa Catalina mountains of Arizona. Our first sight of the Saguaro cactus was at sunset, with a stunning view of a group of cactis on top of a hill, silhouetted against the darkening evening sky.
